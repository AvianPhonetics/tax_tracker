Freelancer's Tax Deduction Tracker
------------------------------------
1. Unzip the folder anywhere on your computer or phone.
2. Open "tax-tracker.html" in your browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge).
3. Start adding income and expenses. All data stays on your device.
4. For best experience on phone, add the page to your home screen
   (browser menu → "Add to Home Screen").
5. Use the "Scan Receipt" button to capture receipts with your camera.
